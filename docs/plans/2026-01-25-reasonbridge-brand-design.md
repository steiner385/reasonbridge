## Logo Concept

Two overlapping circles representing different perspectives, with the intersection highlighted - the "common ground" where understanding happens.

```
     ●●●●●●●         ●●●●●●●
   ●●      ●●●●●●●●●●      ●●
  ●●     ●●●░░░░░░░●●●     ●●
  ●●    ●●░░░░░░░░░░░●●    ●●
  ●●     ●●●░░░░░░░●●●     ●●
   ●●      ●●●●●●●●●      ●●
     ●●●●●●●         ●●●●●●●

   [Left]   [Overlap]   [Right]
    Teal    Light Blue  Soft Blue
```

**Symbolism:**

- Two circles = two perspectives/viewpoints
- Overlap = common ground, shared understanding
- The highlighted intersection = the goal of every discussion

**Usage:**

- Full logo: Symbol + "ReasonBridge" wordmark
- App icon: Symbol only (overlapping circles)
- Favicon: Simplified overlap symbol

## Design Principles

1. **Warm but trustworthy** - Friendly without being childish
2. **Clarity over cleverness** - Communication should be crystal clear
3. **Balanced** - Visual design reflects the mission of finding middle ground
4. **Accessible** - WCAG AA minimum, AAA where possible
5. **Consistent** - Same patterns repeated, build familiarity
